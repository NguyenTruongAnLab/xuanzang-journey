About Xuanzang's Journey

This interactive map shows the remarkable 16-year journey (629-645 CE) of the Chinese Buddhist monk Xuanzang, who traveled from China to India and back to bring Buddhist scriptures to China.

Key Facts:

  • Journey Duration: 16 years (629-645 CE)
  • Total Distance: Over 16,000 km
  • Purpose: To obtain Buddhist scriptures from India
  • Legacy: His journey inspired "Journey to the West"
